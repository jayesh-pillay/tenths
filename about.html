OUR PHILOSOPHY

About Tenths

Productivity is not about doing more; it is about intentional architecture. At Tenths, we believe in the physical sensation of order—where every task is an artifact and every deadline is a milestone in your personal archive.

Why Tenths?

Optimize Time

Slice your day into precise increments. Our tactile interface encourages focus through visual weight and intentional spacing.

Boost Productivity

Move beyond the mission. Build a structural archive of achievements that feel as permanent as limestone.

Build Discipline

Consistent rituals lead to extraordinary results. Our archive maps your journey with an intent and precision.

THE WORKFLOW

Crafting Your First Task

A step-by-step guide to mastering the archive. Precision starts with the first entry.

01

Initiate Archive

Click the '+ New Task' button. This opens a focused canvas, cleared of all distractions to allow your intent to take shape.

02

Define Parameters

Assign a clear title and context. Use our archival tagging system to categorize the effort under the correct project pillar.

03

Set Temporal Bounds

Establish a deadline. Not as a point of stress, but as a boundary of commitment. The interface will reflect its urgency through tonal shifts.

04

Commit & Execute

Seal the task. It is now part of your digital legacy. Track its progress through our tactile progress bars until completion.

Lend Us Your Voice

Your feedback is the chisel that refines our archive. Reach out with thoughts or inquiries.
